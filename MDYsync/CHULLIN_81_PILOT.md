## Alignment procedure

The app starts with placeholder spacing because no trustworthy public transcript was available during packaging. Do not publish those placeholder timestamps.

Use the rapid marker controls:

- Play or scrub to the beginning of the selected Gemara segment.
- Press `M` to record its start time.
- The previous segment's end is set to the same timestamp.
- The marker automatically advances to the next segment.
- Use `[` and `]` to move the marker backward and forward.
- Click a Gemara segment to select it and seek to its current timestamp.
- Save the browser draft frequently, then export the finished JSON.

After the first pass, inspect repeats, digressions, Rashi/Tosafos discussion, and transitions between 81a and 81b in the full editor.
