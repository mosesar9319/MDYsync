Prototype guide

How to test DafSync

  1. Press play on the demo video and watch the daf highlight move.
  2. Drag the timeline. The hover preview and highlighted phrase update immediately.
  3. Click a phrase on the daf to jump the video to that point.
  4. Press “Load Chullin 81 pilot” to open the supplied YouTube shiur and its daf.
  5. While listening, select a phrase and press M to mark its starting time and advance.
  6. Open the synchronization editor to correct timestamps and export the result.

YouTube playback is controlled through YouTube’s embedded player. Automatic transcription and semantic alignment will run in a separate processing service in the production version.
