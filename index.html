Prototype guide

How to test DafSync

  1. The Chullin 81 pilot loads automatically. Play from 12:50 and watch the highlighted phrase change.
  2. Drag the timeline. The hover preview and highlighted phrase update immediately.
  3. Click a phrase on the daf to jump the video to that point.
  4. Press “Load verified sample” to open the supplied YouTube shiur at the manually checked 12:50–13:20 interval.
  5. While listening, select a phrase and press M to mark its starting time and advance.
  6. Open the synchronization editor to correct timestamps and export the result.

YouTube playback is controlled through YouTube’s embedded player. This pilot includes only the manually verified 30-second sample. Unverified caption guesses were removed. Automatic full-video processing will run in a separate production service.
